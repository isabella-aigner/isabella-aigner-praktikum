The Little Fairy Girl

A little girl happy as the world could be
was wandering around the world to see
whatever lies beyond an horizon as which
was lying ahead of her – and she whished
for all creatures of the world to be friends
with no bad feelings and ill intents.

One day she met a small little one
frightened, weak and all alone.
He was lying on a flowerbed
with no way to go anymore ahead.

He told her that he would die very soon
because he was told so by the man in the moon.
He couldn‘t change this fact at all
so he was prepared for his big fall.

The girl turned sad and wanted to cry
for a creature she didn‘t know yet to die
was sadlier than sad and so tears were shed
and the little girl couldn‘t tell anymore what would lie ahead.

The small little one said that it was alright
that his time would come that very night.
He looked into the girls teary eyes
and smiled in a kind and mysterious wise.

But the girl said she wanted to help him so bad
that she didn‘t care anymore what would lie ahead.
She wanted to become acquainted to him
that she didn‘t mind if she became grim.

The little one lying on the flowerbed said
that he was happy with what he once had
but if the girl wanted to help him that much
there would be a way to save him as such.

His name was Elir the girl knows today
a fairy of a land that lies far away.
She welcomed the little one into her heart
so that they will never be ever apart.

In that very moment their souls became one
and nothing else could be anymore done.
The girl herself became one of his kind
leaving her past and everything else behind.

Now she sets out on a journey through pedals and mud
struggling, fighting and pushing forward.
She is trying to find that far away place
which became her reason of living instead.
